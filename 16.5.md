## 1. STRONG ENCRYPTION (MOST IMPORTANT)

|Control|Description|
|---|---|
|WPA3|Recommended security standard|
|WPA2-AES|Acceptable minimum|
|Disable WEP/WPA|Mandatory|

MEMORY HOOK:  
**No WEP. No WPA.**

|---|---|
