
**Attacks = Motive(Goal) + Method(TTP'S) + Vulnerability**
----------------------------------------------------------

TTP'S=
Tactics, techniques and procedures
Tactics = Strategy
Techniques = Technical methods
Procedures = Systematic approach of attack delivery

Vulnerability = existence of weakness in asset that can be exploited

Classification of attacks
-----------------------------------

- Passive - No tampering with devices, like network monitoring .**(Sniffing and eavesdropping)**
- Active - Tampering with data in transit, disrupts communication between systems. **(DoS, MITM, session hijacking, SQL injection)**
- Close-in attacks - When in close proximity of target. **(Shoulder surfing, eavesdropping, dumpster diving)**
- Distribution attacks - tampering with software or hardware before delivery and installation
